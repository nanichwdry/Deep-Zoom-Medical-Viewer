# Medical Images Directory

Place your high-resolution TIF medical images here.

## Required Files:
- sample1.tif
- sample2.tif
- sample3.tif
- sample4.tif
- sample5.tif

## Image Specifications:
- Format: TIFF (.tif)
- Recommended Resolution: 8000 x 6000 px or higher
- Color Mode: RGB or Grayscale
- Compression: LZW or uncompressed

## Sample Sources:
You can obtain medical imaging samples from:
- OpenSlide test images: https://openslide.org/demo/
- Cancer Imaging Archive: https://www.cancerimagingarchive.net/
- NIH Medical Image Database
- Your institution's pathology department (with proper permissions)

## Note:
The viewer will automatically load these images. Ensure proper patient privacy and HIPAA compliance when using real medical data.
